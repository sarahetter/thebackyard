The Backyard showcases the best of the prairie bounty.

Chef Mariana carefully crafts a 4-course meal made with all locally source ingredients. This year, we are hosted at Over the Hill Orchards in Lumsden, SK and will be serving dinner every Saturday of the summer starting May 30th.

We will offer two sittings by reservation only at 5:00pm and 7:30pm. Price per person is $60 (cash only please, tip not included) and includes a non-alcoholic beverage.

Expect a great evening of delicious local food on Dean and Sylvia's beautiful 30 acres of land.

We look forward to feeding you!
